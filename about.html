Why Trust Us?


As someone who has lived in Cork for a whole 20 years, I fell I have some sort of idea what stands out to me as places that need more exposure.


The huge foodie I am and an avid supporter of almost every sport, I think I have an open mind towards new experiences in Cork and want to share my love for certain spaces.

Blarney Castle and Gardens



Blarney Castle and Garden is located in Blarney, and is one of the most famous and beautiful places in Ireland.


Blarney Castle was built in the 1500s and was taken by Queen Elizabeth I, it is a huge castle with huge, tall walls and dungeons.


Don't forget the amazing garden surrounding the castle with 60 acres of surprises, delights and mysteries waiting to be discovered.


The main intrigue is the Blarney Stone!


The Blarney Stone is suppose to have mystical powers of Gab (great eloquence or skill at flattery).

BallyCotton Cliff Walk



BallyCotton Cliff Walk is a personal favourite of mine, found in Ballycotton.


This walk is 7.4 kilometers and is worth the pain and agony of walking that long.


The sites you walk by and the sea could not look any better while you try catch your breathe.


You walk through many different places such as Ballytrasna, Ballyandreen and Cloyne.

English Market



The English Market is located at Princes St, Centre and has been trading for over 200 years.


This is central to Corks Food culture and ofcourse is a need to go. The Market has everything you can imagine including bakerys, cafes, restaurants and much much more!


This is a place for literaly everyone as there are so much too choose from.

Reardens



Reardens is found 3 mins from the English Market at 26 Washington St, Centre and is known for having an Old Irish pub atmosphere.


Reardens has good food, a grea varation of drinks and live music.


They have a "Secret Garden". This is where all the live music and Djs are found 7 days a week!


They also have "The Hidden Attic" known for being a more traditional bar if the Secret Garden is not your tyope of scene.

They have something for everyone with they way that have so much variation across the everything, from drinks to food. More info HERE!


Voodoo Rooms



Voodoo Rooms is a nightclub on 74 Oliver Plunkett St, Centre with live music, djs and entertainment.


This place is very popular for twenty-something year olds all around Cork.


It is a two-story nightclub with plenty of space to dance all night with a group of friends.


Voodoo Rooms is not an easy venue to get into so make sure to show up early and line-up with the rest of the town!

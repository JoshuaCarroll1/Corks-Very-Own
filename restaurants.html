COQBULL





Coqbull is a chicken and Burger restaurant found in 5th French Chruch, Cork City.


Don't let that keep you away from their Delicious donuts with a side of almost any chocolate you could imagine.


For me, this is a need to go place, the food is amazing and staff are so welcoming.


Its as close to the city center without all the noise that comes with that.

Lab 82





Lab 82 is a beautiful diner on 82 Lower Glanmire Rd, Victorian Quarter where you can go and have Pancakes, Burgers, Brownies, and Brunch.


The desserts and breakfast here are to die for! Staff are unbelievably kidn and helpful. No need to reserve a seat as its all walk-ins.


The only problem I can find with this place is the opening hours.


I can honestly say I have never been disappointed while being served here.

Tequila Jacks





Tequila Jacks is located in City Quarter, Lapp's Quay and is one of the most unique and original restaurants you can find.


It serves excellent Mexican Food and in my opinion is a must see for people who are here to try something new and creative.


What makes this place so unique is the domes outside you can dine in right outside the restaurant, towards the river side.

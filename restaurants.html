COQBULL





Coqbull is a chicken and Burger restaurant found in 5th French Chruch, Cork City.


Don't let that keep you away from their Delicious donuts with a side of almost any chocolate you could imagine.


For me, this is a need to go place, the food is amazing and staff are so welcoming.


Its as close to the city center without all the noise that comes with that.

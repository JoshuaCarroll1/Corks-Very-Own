Ballyhass Adventure Group



Ballyhass Adventure Group has two locations, one in Mallow and the other in Coachford. Both locations are huge leisure centers made for big family/ student events.


This is one for people who love to do something a bit adventurous and new, this place is water themed for those who like to get out of their comfort zone.


It has everything you want including a Sauna, wakeboarding, axe throwing, aquaparks and much more.


I believe if you ever decide to leave your house and can only go one place, this is the place to be.

Trabolgan Holiday Village



Trabolgan is found at Rathmore Lane and is a massive Leisure Center.


Trabolgan has Mini-golf, swimming pools/ classes, tennis courts, zip lining and kart racing!


Also you can stay in the village for the weekend with friends and family.


Only downside is it hasnt been seen huge investment of recent and feels a tad out of date compared to some places, this doesnt take away from an amazing time but could add to it.


Perks



You can find Perks Family Entertainment & Indoor Funfair in Seafield, Youghal and is another Leisure center although this is the indoor for all those who hate the outside world.


Perks is known for hosting Birthday Parties and scholl tours across Cork for as long as I can remember.


Perks has entertainment for all ages, for adults you have the Casino section, teenagers you have bowling and laser tag. Children have everything you can imagine from a Jungle gym to bumper cars!


Perks also has food to offer for parties of all sizes.
